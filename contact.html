Oxford Balls is brought to you by three Oxford students with the aim of centralising information about upcoming balls, making it easier to decide which ones to go to. We will try to keep the information as up to date as possible, but can't do a perfect job so please let us know if you spot any mistakes or would like your college ball listed. We can be reached at contact@oxfordballs.co.uk. Please let your friends know about the website!
